Our Goals

Empower Artists

Provide tools for independent artists to publish, monetize, and connect with fans without intermediaries through a hybrid technology (Web 2 + Web 3).

Reward Users

Offer XP and ICE tokens rewards for listening to AI music, engaging with the platform, sharing with friends ensuring every interaction counts and is rewardable.

Build Community

Foster a global community where music creators and lovers as well as content makers collaborate, earn, grow and even have fun together.




Our Mission

At Thundra Music, we aim to empower artists and listeners using AI & Blockchain technologies by creating a fair, transparent, and rewarding music ecosystem. We believe in breaking down barriers in the music industry in making everyone enjoy AI Music while earning, giving independent artists a platform to thrive and users a voice in shaping the future of music.




Key Features

Listen & Publish

Stream music or publish your own tracks with ease.

Thundra Market

Buy and sell artist collections using ICE token.

Earn Rewards

Gain XP and ICE tokens through engagement and convert them for real value.

Community Spaces

Join our social environment through chats, groups, and audio spaces to connect with others.




Our Motivation

The traditional music industry forgets consumers and often leaves artists struggling for fair compensation and exposure. We're motivated to change that by leveraging technology like AI to create a social decentralized platform where creativity is celebrated, and where every user has a stake in the ecosystem.
