Express Yourself Freely

Turn Thundra Market into your personal stage, where you can showcase your art, music, and personality. Upload videos, audio, or digital assets, and engage your community with live updates, much like a reality TV narrative, fostering a unique bond with fans.

Decentralized Security with Blockchain

Leverage cutting-edge Blockchain technology to ensure every transaction and interaction is secure, transparent, and free from middlemen. Your intellectual property and fan connections are protected with immutable records, giving you full control.

Flexible Monetization

Sell your work using ICE tokens, offer limited-edition drops, or give items away for free to build your audience. Set your prices, and allow fans to resell, with Thundra Team taking only 10% to fuel ecosystem growth.

Boost with Thundra Ads

Promote your shows, interviews, or programs directly to your community using Thundra Ads. Reach a wider audience, enhance visibility, and drive engagement with targeted campaigns tailored to your artistic vision.

Direct Fan Engagement

Use Chat & Group Spaces to interact privately and securely with fans. Share behind-the-scenes content, host Q&A sessions, or collaborate on projects, strengthening your community without intermediaries.

Empower Resellers

Choose trusted users to resell your event tickets or show passes. Negotiate prices with them or allow external sales for profit maximization, with details expanded in the Community section.

Be your own boss with the Web 3 Revolution! Thundra Learning offers comprehensive training to master our ecosystem, helping you become a successful, engaged artist. With only 10% of transactions supporting future advancements, you keep the majority of your earnings securely.
