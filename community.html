Your Rewards and Opportunities

Thundra Music rewards your every move! The more you engage, the more you earn, starting with 15 XP per action—inviting friends (doubles with each friend’s join), sharing your referral link, listening to music, liking or downloading tracks, joining Chat & Group Spaces, buying subscriptions or Thundra Ads, participating in Thundra Market, or reselling artist tickets. Earn ICE tokens based on your contribution level, pace, and loyalty, with earnings scaling as you rise through tiers.

Earn XP & ICE Tokens

Accumulate 15 XP per interaction, with bonuses for inviting friends (e.g., 30 XP per friend who joins). ICE tokens reward your devotion, convertible for real value, and grow with your tier progression—loyalty pays off!

Resell with Web 2 Payments

Resell artist tickets using PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, UnionPay, or Mobile Money via Flutterwave. Receive a QR code for payments, ticket printing, or sharing outside the ecosystem, maximizing your profit.

Become a Web 3 Moderator

Join our decentralized moderation team to uphold free speech (barring drug promotion, pornography, or hate speech). Flag content for review—isolated during checks—requiring 10 moderator approvals to act, with non-moderator input possible. Earn rewards and guide the community.

Moderator Perks

Enjoy rewards, direct Thundra Team interaction, artist guidance, and giveaways (with Admin approval). Training is provided, and selection hinges on devotion, engagement, loyalty, personality, and community love—prove your worth!

Thundra Learning Hub

Master the ecosystem with our all-in-one training space. Learn to maximize rewards, engage effectively, and grow your presence, tailored for both users and aspiring moderators.

Join the Thundra Team

Top moderators may be invited (not requested) to join as Staff, embarking on a journey in the Blockchain & AI Revolution with enhanced roles and benefits.

Your engagement shapes our ecosystem! As you level up, your XP earnings grow, with ICE token rewards at the discretion of moderators and the Thundra Team. Embrace a fair, inclusive community where your voice matters.
